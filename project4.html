British Airways Passenger Reviews Dashboard in Tableau

This Interactive Dashboard shows an exploration of passenger reviews for British Airways, spanning from March 2016 to December 2023. Each review includes valuable information such as the date, country, seat type, recommendation, star rating, and type of traveler.

This analysis can help stakeholders gain insights into customer satisfaction, identify trends, and make informed, data driven decisions to enhance the airline experience. improve services, optimize routes, and enhance in-flight amenities.
